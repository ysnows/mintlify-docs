## Overview

Dictation transforms your speech into text with high accuracy, allowing you to dictate text, issue commands, and interact with AI using your voice. It's perfect for hands-free operation, long-form dictation, and accessibility needs.



## Features & Activating

| Feature                | How to Trigger                                 | Description                                    |
|------------------------|------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Dictation              | Press the shortcut key (e.g. `⌥ V`), press again to finish transcription | General voice-to-text dictation                |
| Push to Dictation      | Hold the `Fn` key to start, release to finish transcription | Quick, intuitive input for short dictation     |
| Dictation In SmartBar  | Trigger inside SmartBar for direct Agent interaction      | Voice-driven actions and conversations in SmartBar |


## Using Dictation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate">
    Triggered in a specific way
  </Step>
  <Step title="Speak">
    Speak clearly at a normal pace. A visual indicator shows EnConvo is listening.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel">
    Cancel the transcription by pressing the `Esc` key .
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    Complete the transcription using the corresponding method.
  </Step>
</Steps>


<Tip>
Set your preferred language in Settings → Dictation → Primary Language. EnConvo can also auto-detect languages.
</Tip>

## Tips for Better Recognition

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Speak clearly">
    Maintain a consistent pace and enunciate clearly. Avoid mumbling or speaking too quickly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reduce background noise">
    Use in a quiet environment or use a directional microphone. Close windows and doors if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use voice commands for punctuation">
    Say "period", "comma", "question mark" for punctuation, or enable auto-punctuation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Train your voice (optional)">
    Some models improve with use. Correct transcription errors to help the system learn your voice.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>


## Privacy

<Note>
Voice data is processed according to your AI provider settings. For maximum privacy, use local LLM models for voice command processing.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Microphone not detected">
    1. Check System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone
    2. Ensure EnConvo has microphone permission
    3. Verify microphone is selected in EnConvo settings
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Poor transcription accuracy">
    1. Check microphone quality and positioning
    2. Reduce background noise
    3. Try a different language/accent setting
    4. Speak more slowly and clearly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
